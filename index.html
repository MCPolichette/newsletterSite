Save Nevada Special Education

We are a grassroots activist group fighting to prevent changes to special education programs in Nevada.

About Us

We are a group of parents, educators, and concerned citizens who are dedicated to preserving special education services for children in Nevada. Our mission is to ensure that all students with disabilities have access to the services and accommodations they need to succeed in school and in life.

Through community organizing, advocacy, and education, we are working to prevent changes to special education programs that would negatively impact the lives of children with disabilities and their families.
